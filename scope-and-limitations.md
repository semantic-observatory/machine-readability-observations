# Scope and limitations

This repository deliberately operates within a constrained scope.
Understanding these boundaries is essential to avoid misinterpretation.

---

## What is within scope

- Interpretation of public web entities by AI systems.
- Effects of semantic clarity on summarization behavior.
- Observable differences across AI models.

---

## What is out of scope

- Search engine ranking analysis.
- Traffic measurement or attribution.
- Commercial performance or ROI evaluation.
- Validation of consulting methods or services.

---

## No guarantees

This repository does not claim:

- guaranteed AI citation,
- stable interpretation over time,
- immunity from model updates,
- superiority of any specific architecture.

Any such inference would be incorrect.

---

## Non-transferability

Observations documented here:

- apply only to the specific contexts described,
- should not be generalized to unrelated entities,
- may not apply across industries, languages, or geographies.

---

## Intended use

This repository is intended for:

- analytical reading,
- comparative understanding,
- interpretability research.

It is not intended to guide purchasing decisions or technical implementations.
